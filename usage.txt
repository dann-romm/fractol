Fractol - This project is meant to create graphically beautiful fractals

usage: ./fractol <argument>

Arguments:
   -m [power]			Mandelbroth fractal specified power 2 to 5
   -j [power] <x> <y>	Julia fractal specified power 2 to 5
   -s					Burning ship fractal
   -n					Newton fractal specified power 2 to 5
   -h					Print Help (this message) and exit
